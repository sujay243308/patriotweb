    Financial Aid Award Offer and Acceptance Procedure
    The steps required to complete the acceptance of your financial aid award offer are listed below. Please be sure to accept or decline each award type listed in your financial aid offer as soon as possible as some awards are limited and based on the availability of these funds.
    1. Review the information provided on the Award Offer page to see what information was used to determine your financial aid eligibility.

    2. Review the Resource page for Terms and Conditions as well as the Terms and Conditions information in the FINANCIAL AID HANDBOOK, located on the Office of Student Financial Aid website.

    3. On the Award Offer page, review the awards that have been offered and complete one of the following options:
      • Accept all awards for the amounts offered
      • Accept, Modify or Decline individual awards

    4. Once you have accepted or declined your award offer(s), select SUBMIT.
    No actions are required at this time.
